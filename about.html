About

Moonstruck Cultivation is a project initiated by editor Kosuke Ide and Zensei Shinsha. Beginning in August 2026, it creates a real space for encounters across art, craft, food, design, care, nature, community, music, play, philosophy, literature, science, and film.

Rather than gathering around a fixed discipline or theme, we invite people to bring what they value: an object, a book, a sound, an idea, a practice, or a question. Through sharing, listening, and looking closely, familiar things may begin to appear otherwise.

where touching becomes thinking,
and knowing becomes feeling.

Our gatherings take place at the Haruchika Noguchi Memorial Music Room in Komae, Tokyo, with the cooperation of Zensei Shinsha.
